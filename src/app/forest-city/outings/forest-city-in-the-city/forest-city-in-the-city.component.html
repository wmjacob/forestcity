Forest City In The City

"Forest City In The City" are Forest City Lodge No. 388's informal social gathering events.
Building Brotherhood

Come join us for fun, fellowship, and refreshments.

Come as you are, and get to know the Brethren of Forest City Lodge outside of the Lodge!

All Are Welcome!

These events are open to the general public, not only Freemasons.

Feel free to bring your friends, family, children, significant others...anyone!

All Events Are Pay-your-own-way

These events are informal and not sponsored by the Lodge.
