Have Questions?

If you have general questions about our Lodge or Freemasonry, please fill out the form and we will reach out to you as soon as we can.

Come Meet Us

We meet on the 1st and 3rd Wednesdays of the month at 7:30pm, with the exception of July and August.

Before our meetings, we usually have a meal, at around 6:30pm. If you would like to join us for a meal to meet and eat with the brethren, please RSVP on our Upcoming Events page.
